Why estate agents lose leads

Most estate agencies still rely on static web forms, voicemail and email. Research shows that:

  • Up to 40% of buyer enquiries arrive outside 9–5
  • About 48% of sales enquiries are never answered and response times average almost 4 hours
  • Over 85% of callers who hit voicemail never call back
  • Form completion rates are low, and almost half of real estate forms are abandoned

These numbers translate into thousands of pounds lost to missed viewings and instructions.

The cost of friction

Clients expect instant answers and flexible booking. When forced to fill a long form or wait for a call back, they move on.
